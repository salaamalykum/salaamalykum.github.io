\nWhat is Muslim Free Trade Association?\n\n\n
 \n\n\n
Muslim Free Trade Association is for business owners in different muslim communities.  \n\n\n
 \n\n\n
\nWhat is the Muslim Free Trade Association doing?\n\n\n
 \n\n\n
We are building muslim factory owners and entrepreneur alliance. Let more muslim factory owners and small business owners eliminate business difficulties. Let more products made by muslims can be exported to the EU, North America, etc.\n\n\n
 \n\n\n
\nWhat we trying to do:\n\n\n
 \n\n\n
